Red Velvet Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 eggs room temperature
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • Red food coloring

Cream cheese frosting

  • 3 sticks unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 confectioners sugar
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch kosher salt

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Place cupcake papers in a cupcake pan.
    2. In a large bowl, sift the dry ingredients together (including the sugar). Give it a whisk.
    3. In another bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients until combined. (The batter may be clumpy! Do not worry!).
    4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients. Mix until combined.
    5. Add the red food coloring until you reach the desired color.
    6. Distribute the batter evenly into cupcake papers.
    7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden and springy to the touch.

    Cream cheese frosting directions

    1. Cream the butter. Sift confectioners sugar into a large bowl.
    2. Add room temperature cream cheese to the creamed butter. Add vanilla and a pinch of salt. Beat together.
    3. Add the confectioners sugar in cups. Mix until combined. If you’re wanting a stronger buttercream, keep adding more sifted confectioners sugar until it’s stiff.
    4. Transfer buttercream to a piping bag, snip off the tip and add a large dollop on top of each cupcake.
